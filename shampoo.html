Product Information

Ingredients, directions, and warnings

Ingredients

Water (Aqua), Decyl Glucoside, Coco-Glucoside, Sodium C14-16 Olefin Sulfonate, Sodium Lauroyl Sarcosinate, Aloe Barbadensis Leaf Juice, Glycerin, Sodium Lactate, Panthenol, Allantoin, Tetrasodium Glutamate Diacetate, Gluconolactone, Sodium Benzoate, Potassium Sorbate, Citric Acid.

Directions for Use

Wet hair and hands thoroughly. Dispense 2-4 pumps of shampoo directly into your palm or onto the scalp. Gently massage into the roots and through the hair for 60-90 seconds. For days when flakiness is noticeable, leave on for an additional minute before rinsing. Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Use 2-3 times per week or as needed for best results.

Warnings

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes. In case of contact with eyes, rinse immediately with plenty of water. If irritation or rash occurs, discontinue use and consult a physician. Keep out of reach of children. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
